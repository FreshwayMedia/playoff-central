Pool Rules

Entry Requirements

  • Entry fee: $20 per team
  • Maximum of 2 teams per person
  • All entries must be submitted before the start of the first playoff game

Team Selection

Each participant must select:

  • 12 Forwards
  • 6 Defensemen
  • 2 Goalies

No more than 3 players from any single NHL team are allowed.

Scoring System

Category Points
Goals 1
Assists 1
Game-Winning Goals 1
Goalie Wins 2
Goalie Shutouts 3

Playoff Bonuses

  • Conference Final Appearance: 5 points per player
  • Stanley Cup Final Appearance: 10 points per player
  • Stanley Cup Win: 15 points per player

Prize Distribution

  • 1st Place: 50% of the pot
  • 2nd Place: 30% of the pot
  • 3rd Place: 20% of the pot

Tiebreakers

In the event of a tie, the following criteria will be used in order:

  1. Most goals scored
  2. Most assists
  3. Most game-winning goals
  4. Most goalie wins
  5. Most goalie shutouts
